The Best Christmas Movies According to Brandon

My top 4 Christmas movies of all-time.

Elf

Zany with nonstop laughter. Will Ferrell at his best.

National Lampoon's Christmas Vacation

Comical with great chemistry between characters.

How the Grinch Stole Christmas

The animated and the live-action remake. They are both excellent and everyone should watch both of them...
But, if you are only going to watch one, then make it the original animated masterpiece.

A Charlie Brown Christmas

Heartwarming. You are never too old for this classic.
